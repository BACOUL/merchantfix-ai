# AI-first SEO structure

MerchantFix.ai SEO pages now include an AI-first layer designed for search engines, AI assistants, and answer engines.

## Added structure

The SEO cluster now includes:

- Direct answer blocks near the top of key pages.
- Short FAQ sections written in question-and-answer format.
- FAQPage JSON-LD.
- BreadcrumbList JSON-LD.
- Clear limits: no Google approval guarantee and no invented product data.

## Pages covered

The AI-first layer is applied to:

- `/google-merchant-center-errors-shopify`
- `/fix/google-merchant-center-errors/[slug]`
- `/fix/shopify-google-shopping/[slug]`

## Purpose

The goal is to make MerchantFix.ai easier to understand for:

- Google Search
- AI Overviews
- ChatGPT browsing-style summaries
- Perplexity-style answer engines
- Gemini-style answer engines
- merchant copy-paste searches

## Positioning to preserve

MerchantFix.ai helps Shopify merchants diagnose Google Merchant Center product data issues.

It does not:

- connect to Shopify admin in V1
- connect to Google Merchant Center in V1
- guarantee approval
- guarantee ranking, traffic, performance, or sales
- invent GTIN, MPN, brand, price, shipping, or product facts
- treat misrepresentation as an automatic CSV fix
