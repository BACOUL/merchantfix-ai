# Product Data Readiness Strategy

## Current wedge

MerchantFix.ai starts with a narrow, practical wedge: Shopify merchants dealing with Google Merchant Center product data issues.

The current MVP remains limited to Shopify URL surface scan and Shopify CSV diagnostic for GTIN, MPN, brand, identifier_exists, and simple visible product data risks.

## Why product data readiness matters

Shopping channels depend on clear product data to understand, match, classify, and trust products.

For merchants, weak product data can create avoidable friction: missing identifiers, unclear brands, weak titles, thin descriptions, missing prices, missing images, and manual review work.

MerchantFix.ai should help merchants find those issues without inventing identifiers or making unsupported approval promises.

## Long-term opportunity

If the Shopify and Google Merchant Center wedge is validated, MerchantFix.ai can expand into a broader product data readiness platform.

That future may include readiness for Google Shopping, marketplaces, comparison engines, and AI-assisted shopping surfaces where structured, accurate product data becomes increasingly important.

This is a strategic direction, not a current MVP expansion.

## Strategic rule

The product must move one validated step at a time.

MerchantFix.ai should only expand to new channels, integrations, monitoring, paid workflows, or AI-assisted explanations after earlier versions prove demand, reliability, and manageable support.

MerchantFix.ai must never guarantee approval, ranking, marketplace acceptance, traffic, sales, or AI assistant visibility.

## Positioning ladder

V0.5: no-install Shopify URL surface scan for visible product data risks.

V1: Shopify CSV diagnostic for Google Merchant Center identifier issues.

V2: paid Fix Pack only after V1 is validated.

V3: broader Merchant Center error families only after paid validation.

V4: agency workflow only after repeated professional usage is validated.

Long-term platform: product data readiness across shopping channels only after the earlier steps prove the need.
